Eisenhower Matrix:
• Urgent and Important: Do these tasks first.
• Important, but Not Urgent: Schedule a specific time to do these tasks.
• Urgent, but Not Important: See if you can delegate these tasks.
• Neither Urgent nor Important: These can be postponed or eliminated.
The 80/20 Rule (Pareto Principle):
• Focus on the 20% of tasks that yield 80% of the results.
• Identify high-impact tasks and tackle them first.
